    Modern Art Collector

    Nancy

    42

    San Francisco, CA

    Highly tech-savvy

    Marketing Executive

    Back Story

    1. •Nancy lives with her husband in a modest home in San Francisco.

    2. •She has always been passionate about modern art, often attending art fairs or exhibitions whenever possible.

    3. •Her homes reflects her love for art, filled as it is with innovative and boundary-pushing artwork.

    Motivations

    1. •She wants to stay ahead of art trends and add the latest works to her collection. 

    2. •She wants to find a convenient and immersive way to experience and purchase art without disrupting her busy schedule.

    3. •She wants to potentially be able to show her husband the art she would be purchasing while also discovering new modern artists.

    Frustrations

    1. •Nancy travels frequently for work, which leaves little time to visit art galleries and exhibitions in person.

    2. •She feels screenshots and online images often fail to capture the true essence and details of the artwork.

    3. •Art fairs and exhibitions are often held in other cities, making it logistically and financially challenging to attend.

    Ideal Experience

    1. •Nancy would like the virtual platform to allow her to view the art pieces from all angles, providing a realistic sense of scale and detail.

    2. •She would like the ability to participate in online art exhibitions and fairs from her home.

    3. •She would like access to detailed information about the artists and artwork to make imformed decisions.
