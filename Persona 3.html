    Back Story

    1. •Charles as a history professor has a deep appreciation for traditional art.

    2. •His home has some antique scultupes and artifacts that reflect his passion for history and art.

    3. •He is very active online, discussing the intricacies of modern against traditional art, occasionally with a former student.

    Motivations

    1. •Charles would like to expand his collection with authentic traditional art pieces that also have historical value.

    2. •He would enjoy a more seamless and trustworthy art-buying experience,

    Frustrations

    1. •Charles is limited by limited local high-quality traditional art exhibitions and auctions.

    2. •He finds it difficult to verify the authenticity of art pieces when purchasing online.

    3. •He finds the physical strain combined with the time commitment of traveling to be quite tedious.

    Ideal Experience

    1. •Charles would like a virtual platform with a detailed and authentic viewing experience of traditional artworks.

    2. •He would like a secure and reliable online purchasing transaction with verified information of the location and authenticity of the pieces.

    3. •He would like virtual access to exclusive auctions and exhibitions, allowing him to participate and bid on artworks without the need to travel.
